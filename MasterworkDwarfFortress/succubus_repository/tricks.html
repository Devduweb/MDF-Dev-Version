Tips and Tricks

1. Upgrading your fighters

Elite succubi are not only excellent dodgers and strickers, they can also bring powerful spells to the field. You can use the workshop profile (shift + P) to select who gets the upgrade. They do not require an enabled job.

  • While Gehenna fire provides good fire attacks, Torrid kiss is a free action and will burn your enemies without interrupting melee attacks.
  • Berserk rage is more effective if the fighter is miserable but still have effect if the fighter is happy. It can be combined with Martial Excitation.
  • Learning the secrets of lust provide area of effect buffs and dimming interactions. At regular intervals, the courtesans will blink, meaning that their combat abilities are multiplied.

2. Clearing sieges

It is important to clear sieges before late spring to allow your civilization's caravan to visit you, otherwise you will not receive migrants for a year. There is a few rituals that can help you :

  • Sow discord (6 souls) will cause infighting among the invaders. This is the quickest way to get rid of a squad of enemies.
  • Lure invaders (1 soul) will break camps and call all the invaders into your traps.
  • Conjure fire storm (temple of fire, 1 soul) have a decent chance to burn enemies outside. It is especially good if your fighters are fireborn.

3. Fire immunity

The temple of fire provides fire - and cold - immunity. You can use that to channel magma, work on glaciers, throw burning attacks safely or play in the forest fires.

If a succubus can be fire immune, her clothes will not be. To avoid accidents, military uniforms made of fire safe materiel can help you. Or you can assign an empty uniform in 'replace clothing' mode, the succubi are much less affected by the lack of clothing. 

Do not forget that you can still drown in magma.

4. Evil biomes

On the surface of evil biomes, you can pick black roses and bloodworms. Wine made from those plants will have positive effect on your succubi.

If your civilization has settled on evil biomes, you will also be able to buy those plants from the carvans.

5. Adventuring as a succubus

There is plenty of caste choices as an adventurer. Half breed all have their perks, and fireborn succubi can swim in magma. Use the gender selection before starting your adventures to switch castes.

If you look at the civilization's border before starting, you will likely find forts with succubi soldiers to recruit inside. After completing a few quest, the local lady will give you more tasks, allowing you to work for your civilization instead of humans.
