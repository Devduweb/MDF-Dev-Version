Tilesets

Tilesets add graphics to DF, simple as that. You can still play in ASCII, in a dozen ascii-like sets, which use a similar style but different icons, or with any of 10 different graphical sets. Installation is very straight forward, you simply pick it in the GUI. You need to generate a new world after this.

MDF currently offers 25 tilesets, the previews of which you can see here. I didnt post those with only font/wall changes, (MDF comes in 3 types, and JollyBastion in 2) so you have 21 pics to look at. Some of the tilesets come with custom creatures sprites and custom color schemes as well, which are automatically installed by the GUI. You can still change the color schemes to any you like. Try it out, they really do make a difference!
