You can construct automatons in the clockwork automaton workshop and the automaton body shop. To build an automaton, you'll need a frame created in the Metalsmith's Forge under "other objects", 2 lenses, an acuator, a clockwork device, and an artificial brain from the Tinker's Shop, and a fairly large number of mechanisms. Creating an automaton takes a lot of time and resources, but once it is created, it will serve you well. Even small, low-quality automatons are tough fighters and hard to break, to say nothing of the ones made out of high-level metals like iron or steel. Broken automatons can be dismantled and their broken mechanisms can be repaired in a Tinker's Shop with a 75% success rate, and their frame remains even when broken, so most of the resources that went into making them can be reused.
