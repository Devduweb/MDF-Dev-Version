Masterwork DF Plant Table

In here you can find all plants for the mod, with all infos. They are sorted in normal plants you can encounter, fake plants that are only used for products (for example plump helmet-juice plants, which are just used to give the juice a different ID then the plump helmet-wine has), Kobold Camp plants, Orc Fortress plants, the additional Flora&Fauna plants and the additional Flora&Fauna trees. The last two are quite long and are disabled by default. You can also find already compiled lists for the dfhack getplants command, sorted by aboveground plants, underground plants, aboveground trees and underground trees.

At this point a big thanks to maklak for the script to read these out directly from the raws.
